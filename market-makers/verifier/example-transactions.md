```

  defuse-alpha.near mt_batch_balance_of \
  json-args '{
    "account_id": "defuse-ops.near",
    "token_ids": [
        "nep141:wrap.near",
        "nep141:usdt.tether-token.near"
    ]
  }' network-config mainnet now
```
